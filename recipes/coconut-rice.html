Burmese Coconut Rice

This coconut rice is best served with chicken curry, ngapi kyaw, and roselle leaves.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups uncooked jasmine rice, rinsed and drained
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 Â½ cups water
  • 1 tablespoon grated onion, or to taste
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt

Directions

  1. Heat coconut milk, water, onion, sugar, and salt in a saucepan. Pour in rinsed and drained rice. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and let simmer, stirring occasionally, until fragrant, tender, and liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
